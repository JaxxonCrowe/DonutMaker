WElCOME TO FRED's FANCIES!
Fred has been an avid baker since he was 7 years old, an apprentice of his italian grandmother until he graduated from high school. His grandparents saw Fred's talent and scraped up the funds to open up a shop for Fred and worked right along side him until they could no longer work. Now he's got little shops all over the state and is about to expand nationwide. Take a taste test and tell us what you think! While you are enjoying the fruits of his labor, take some time to play the game and see how fast you can start producing large quantities of donuts for the very hungry people of the city we live in.
